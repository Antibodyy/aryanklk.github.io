Hands-on workshop focused on bicycle maintenance, repair, and mechanical understanding.

## Workshop Content

* Basic bicycle anatomy and components
* Preventive maintenance procedures
* Common repair techniques
* Tool usage and safety
* Troubleshooting mechanical issues

## Skills Developed

* Hands-on mechanical skills
* Problem-solving and diagnostics
* Tool proficiency
* Practical engineering application
* Sustainable transportation advocacy

## Practical Application

Developing practical mechanical skills and understanding of mechanical systems through real-world bicycle repair and maintenance activities.
