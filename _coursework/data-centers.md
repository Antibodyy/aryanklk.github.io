Advanced course exploring thermal management, energy efficiency, and sustainability in modern data center infrastructure.

## Topics Covered

* Data center thermal management and cooling strategies
* Energy efficiency optimization
* Power usage effectiveness (PUE) metrics
* Sustainable infrastructure design
* Heat transfer and fluid mechanics applications
* Advanced cooling technologies (liquid cooling, immersion cooling)

## Course Project

Project focused on thermal management optimization and energy efficiency improvements for data center systems.

## Skills Developed

* Thermal modeling and simulation
* Energy systems analysis
* Computational fluid dynamics (CFD) applications
* Sustainable design principles
