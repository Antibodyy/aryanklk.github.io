Hands-on course focused on consulting with deep technology startups to evaluate market opportunities and product-market fit.

## Course Structure

Direct engagement with emerging technology startups to conduct market research, competitive analysis, and commercialization strategy development.

## Key Activities

* Market research and competitive landscape analysis
* Product-market fit assessment
* Customer discovery and validation
* Go-to-market strategy development
* Technical feasibility evaluation

## Skills Developed

* Market analysis and research methodologies
* Startup consulting and advisory
* Technology commercialization
* Strategic thinking and business development
* Cross-functional team collaboration
