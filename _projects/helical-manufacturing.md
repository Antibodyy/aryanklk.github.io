Developing metrology systems, mechanical improvements, and motion control software for a large-format lithography-based 3D printing system.

## About HeliCAL

HeliCAL is a cutting-edge additive manufacturing system that uses lithography-based projection techniques to enable large-format, high-precision 3D printing. The system combines optical projection with synchronized motion control to build parts with sub-micron accuracy at scales beyond conventional lithography systems.

## My Contributions

* Executing the structural design improvements and hands-on assembly of a large-format lithography-based manufacturing system.
* Leading development of a high-precision metrology setup and worked on tolerancing to ensure sub-micron accuracy.
* Coordinating procurement of mechanical components; creating DFM-optimized part drawings and complex assemblies in SolidWorks.
* Engineered a 4 DOF motion control system using a custom G-Code pipeline (C++) to synchronize hardware with optical projections.
